**Ulrich Moustakas** (University of Tübingen) received the Dr. rer. nat.
in Mathematics in 1985 with the dissertation *Majorisierung und
Spektraleigenschaften positiver Operatoren auf Banachverbänden*
(*Majorization and Spectral Properties of Positive Operators on Banach
Lattices*). Soon thereafter he turned to theology and later habilitated
in Systematic Theology at the University of Tübingen (Privatdozent). His
theological publications include *Theologie im Kontext von
Wissenschaftstheorie und Hermeneutik* (Verlag Dr. Kovač, 2017).
