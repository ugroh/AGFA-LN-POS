**Rainer Nagel** is Professor Emeritus of Mathematics at the University
of Tübingen and a leading authority in functional analysis and operator
semigroup theory. A doctoral student of H. H. Schaefer, he became
internationally recognized for his contributions to one-parameter
semigroups and positivity. He served as editor and coordinating
co-author of *One-Parameter Semigroups of Positive Operators* (Springer
LNM 1184, 1986), a landmark monograph now appearing in its second
edition. His subsequent books include *One-Parameter Semigroups for
Linear Evolution Equations* (with K.-J. Engel, GTM 194, 2000), *A Short
Course on Operator Semigroups* (2006), and *Operator Theoretic Aspects
of Ergodic Theory* (GTM 272, 2015). Professor Nagel co-founded the
*Journal of Evolution Equations*, held editorial leadership at
*Semigroup Forum*, and initiated the *International Internet Seminar on
Evolution Equations* (since 1998). He also founded the *Romseminare* in
1993, an innovative interdisciplinary seminar format in which
mathematics students explore connections between mathematics and other
sciences, the arts, and society---an initiative that continues
successfully to this day.
